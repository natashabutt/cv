Projects That I'm Excited About

Mitigating Gender Bias in Occupation Classification

During my Data Science MSc, I worked on a natural language processing group project. We chose to investigate mitigating gender bias in occupation classification using graph convolutional networks. Our project received over 90% and I have published an article with Towards Data Science on our progress so far. Now we are developing the project further to look at other models such as BERT, RoBERTa and ALBERT, with the aim to submit a paper for publication.

Investigating Stop and Search through Spatial Analysis with Machine Learning Techniques

For my summer MSc thesis, I am investigating racial bias in UK Stop and Search through spatial analysis. This involves a large public stop and search dataset of over 1,000,000 entries. I am carrying out this analysis in R using SpatStat and other geospatial packages. I am also combining other large datasets from the 2011 Census and the Labour Force Survey. Machine learning techniques such as feature extraction will be implemented to improve the efficiency of this analysis.
