Projects That I'm Excited About

Mitigating Gender Bias in Occupation Classification

During my Data Science MSc, I worked on a natural language processing group project. We chose to investigate mitigating gender bias in occupation classification using graph convolutional networks. Our project received over 90% and I have published an article with Towards Data Science on our progress so far. Now we are developing the project further to look at other models such as BERT, RoBERTa and ALBERT.

Bayesian Modelling of UK Stop and Search

For my summer MSc thesis, I looked at Bayesian spatial and non-spatial modelling of UK stop and search. Hierarchical Poisson generalised linear mixed models were fitted using Bayesian inference with Markov Chain Monte Carlo methods. Specifically, I chose Gibbs Sampling for which I used BUGS in combination with R. Spatial operations were performed on the data and spatial structure was introduced through an intrinsic conditional auto-regressive effect. I also published an article on the non-spatial modelling with Towards Data Science.
